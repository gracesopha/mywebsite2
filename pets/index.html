My Pets!

Luna

My dog is a chihuahua-papillion that my family adopted about six years ago. She is currently seven years old and is the first dog I've ever had! Though, she is very anti-social and only enjoys playing with cats.

Aiko

Aiko was the second addition to the family. She was easily accepted by Luna as her new best friend. Aiko and Luna both have a tendency to dislike people, so they get along well. Funnily enough, both our pets have a third eye mark on their forehead and are three different colors , which is a sign of good luck!
